# In All Fairness, a Warning {.unnumbered}

Dear reader, are you prepared? You hold in your hands a true tragedy, worsening with every page turned, or finger swiped. I hardly care how you consume this media, only that you paid for it properly. Life is expensive.

Oh, no, not a tragedy as you no doubt imagine. Though, there is a large amount of destruction, death, humiliation, and highly questionable hair styles, but those are not tragic, merely the natural consequence of men and women given a touch too much power playing gods and goddesses with the world. Though the hairstyles are all Greg.

No no, this tragedy is a much more personal and poignant matter. It causes me to shiver even now. Reader, this is the story about how arguably the greatest mastermind of a generation, a savant with technology, an unlikely eye in the storm of insanity that you are about to explore—more specifically, how I—was an incomparable, inexcusable, absolute imbecile.
